In short about us

Our Agriculture Society

A welfare Society has been established on 8th January, 2011 to look after the welfare of Agriculture students studying at the level of Agriculture.To Bring “Agricultural Administrative Reform Revolution” by uniting all the Youth in Agriculture i.e. Students, AIASA is established with sense of dedication & Essence of patriotism. Now we will fight for our Agriculture, for Justice, For the Farmers, For the Nation, for Every Indian. Now we got our identity; we are here to act as Instrument of god, helping Hand for farmers, to serve and the love the nation unconditionally, to lead the Nation…To feed the nation

From The State Presidents Desk

Mr. Sanoop Skariah Varughese

AIASA is a professional organization established on 8th January 2011 with the approval of Central Government,Ministry of Agriculture, ICAR, Ministry of Consumer Affairs under the societies Registration Act, 1860 and it was formally launched by the then Hon’ble Minister of State for Agriculture on 10th May, 2011 during All India convention on Agriculture Administrative Reforms. The Association works for empowering youth in Agriculture for better professional career. This professional organization is being supported by ICAR, Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare and NABARD for conducting annual youth convention at SAU on rotation basis. On the initiation and proposal of AIASA, Central government has declared Agriculture and allied degrees as professional degrees at par with medical degree. Many Agricultural Universities throughout the country have facilitated in establishment of AIASA Units in their respective campuses and carried out registration of students and faculties to strengthen this professional organization. It has been now almost a year past for me as the State President for AIASA in Kerala. AIASA has been in a rapid fire for the last one year and it’s now established in all Agriculture and Allied Universities of Kerala with bounty memberships. It has been running at a higher pace after each hurdle with renewed wings. Being the State President for the association I would like to admit that a good team work has made us move forward at higher paces each moment. The history of Agriculture in India dates back to Indus Valley Civilization Era and even before that in some parts of Southern India. India ranks second worldwide in farm outputs. As per 2018, Agriculture employed 50% of the Indian work force and contributed 17-18% to country's GDP. In 2016. Agriculture and allied sectors like animal husbandry, forestry and fisheries accounted for 15.4% of the GDP (gross domestic product) with about 31% of the workforce in 2014. The economic contribution of agriculture to India's GDP is steadily declining with the country's broad-based economic growth. As the above paragraph stated India seems to go down in economic contribution from agriculture, even though most of its population depend on Agriculture. But through the trained and skilled efforts of Agriculture and allied sector professionals these problems can be mitigated. If these professionals are not placed at the places where they actually have to, these problems are left unaddressed which infers that the ‘Right person has to be placed at the Right Position’. To attain this cognizance there has to be a proper bond which is to be fostered throughout the country, wherein we can help one another, fight together for our rights, and also support our farming community. As of now, there is only one association throughout the country to integrate students, scientists/professors, agriculture professionals throughout the country which is the All India Agricultural Students Association (AIASA). Being part of the organization and leading the agricultural community in Kerala, I strive to integrate the agricultural fraternity in Kerala.

Mr. Sanoop Skariah Varughese
